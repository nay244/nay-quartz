# Nay

## Who am I?

I'm a Cybersecurity Engineer and Data Analyst with a passion for transforming complex data into meaningful insights while building secure, scalable technology solutions. My background combines cybersecurity operations, analytics, and business intelligence, allowing me to approach problems from both a defensive and data-driven perspective.

Professionally, I work with modern data pipelines and reporting platforms, leveraging tools such as Power BI, Redshift, DBT, and Jenkins to support decision-making and operational efficiency. Alongside analytics, I have a strong interest in cloud technologies, particularly Microsoft Azure, where I continue exploring cloud security, infrastructure, automation, and modern architecture practices.

This blog is a space where I share insights, projects, lessons learned, and ideas across cybersecurity, data analytics, and cloud engineering. Whether it's breaking down technical concepts, discussing emerging technologies, or documenting hands-on experiences, my goal is to continuously learn and contribute to the broader tech community.
