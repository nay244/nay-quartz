## How this garden grows

Notes are written in Markdown, linked with `[[wikilinks]]`, and published automatically via GitHub Actions. The structure is intentionally loose — ideas move between nodes as they mature.

Think of each card on the home page as a **cluster** of related notes, not a rigid category.
